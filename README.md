## Introduction

FreeCodeCamp offers a comprehensive curriculum covering various aspects of web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Databases, APIs, and more. This repository serves as a record of my solutions to the challenges presented in the curriculum.

Feel free to explore the code, learn from it, and compare your solutions with mine. Keep in mind that there are often multiple valid ways to solve a problem, and my solutions may not be the only or best ones.

## Directory Structure

The repository is organized by certification, with each certification containing subdirectories for the challenges and projects associated with it.
